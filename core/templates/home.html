LearnSwift Academia: Integrating Faith, Knowledge, and Skills

Welcome to LearnSwift Academia, a unique Islamic school located in the heart of Agege, Lagos. We are dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where children receive a robust education encompassing both Western academic standards and profound Islamic teachings. Our mission is to cultivate intellectually curious, morally upright, and practically skilled individuals ready to contribute positively to society.

Our Mission & Vision See Our Programs

Why Choose LearnSwift Academia?

Holistic Development

Balancing rigorous academics, essential character-building based on Islamic principles (Tarbiyah), and spiritual growth.

Personalized Attention

Small class sizes and dedicated teachers ensure individual learning needs are met, helping each child reach their unique potential.

Future-Ready Skills

Integrating critical thinking, problem-solving, digital literacy (coding), and practical vocational training for real-world success.

Our Unique Educational Approach

More Than Just a School

We blend the best of modern pedagogy with timeless Islamic wisdom. Our curriculum is designed not just for academic achievement but for building strong moral foundations and practical life skills.

  • Integrated Islamic & Western Curriculum
  • Emphasis on Quran, Sunnah & Arabic
  • STEM & Coding Programs
  • Hands-on Vocational Training
  • Character Development (Akhlaq & Adab)
  • Supportive & Collaborative Community
