2. Swift Aleem Academy (Arabic & Islamic Studies)

For students seeking deeper religious knowledge, this track offers intensive study of Arabic language (Nahw, Sarf, Balagha), Quran memorization (Hifz) and recitation (Tajweed), Hadith, Fiqh, and Seerah.

Key Outcomes:
  • Proficiency in Quran memorization and Tajweed.
  • Mastery of classical and modern standard Arabic.
  • In-depth understanding of core Islamic sciences.
  • Strengthened spiritual connection and practice.
