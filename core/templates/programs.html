Our Programs

At LearnSwift Academia, we believe in nurturing strong character, Islamic values, and practical skills to prepare students for success in every aspect of life.

1. LearnSwift Academia

Primary and Secondary Western and Islamic School. Here, students excel in core academic subjects while grounding themselves in strong Islamic values. We integrate faith, character-building, and 21st-century skills to ensure holistic growth.

  • Certificates/Outcomes: Primary School Certifiate (Common Entrance), Basic Education Certificate (Junior), Senior Secondary Certificate (WAEC, NECO etc), Strong Moral Foundation.
  • Students' Achievement: Students develop a balanced worldview, academic excellence, and ethical leadership skills.
  • Parents' Rewards: Parents see their children grow into confident, responsible individuals.

2. Swift Aleem Academy

A strictly Arabic and Islamic school focused on Arabic language mastery, Quran memorization, and Islamic sciences. Perfect for those who wish to deepen their faith-based knowledge.

  • Certificates/Outcomes: Quran Memorization Certificate, Proficiency in Arabic Language, Islamic Sciences Mastery.
  • Students' Achievement: Students gain profound spiritual growth and command of Arabic, while mastering Islamic sciences.
  • Parents' Rewards: Parents witness the strengthening of their childâ€™s faith and moral compass.

3. Vocational Academy

An embedded program within LearnSwift, offering hands-on vocational training. Students gain practical skills in trades such as carpentry, fashion design, culinary arts, or other local crafts, empowering them with entrepreneurial abilities.

  • Certificates/Outcomes: Vocational Skills Certificate, Portfolio of Practical Projects.
  • Students' Transformation: Students become resourceful and self-reliant. They excel in their chosen trades and become future entrepreneurs.
  • Parents' Rewards: Parents witness their children becoming productive and self-sustaining.

4. Coding Academy

A specialized program that imparts coding skills in various stacks and in-demand digital technologies. Students learn problem-solving, web development, app creation, and more.

  • Certificates/Outcomes: Coding Certificates (Frontend, Backend, Full-stack), Tech Portfolio.
  • Students' Transformation: Graduates are ready for the digital workforce, building apps and websites.
  • Parents' Rewards: Parents witness their children becoming future-ready innovators and success in the digital world.

5. LearnSwift Online Academy

Offering all the above programs and more through a convenient online platform. Students can learn at their own pace from anywhere in the world while maintaining strong Islamic values.

  • Certificates/Outcomes: Flexible Online Completion Certificates, Digital Skills Accreditation.
  • Achievement: Families save time and resources, and students gain the freedom to learn in a flexible environment without compromising on educational quality.
